Chef Evi Catering & Events

We are a boutique catering company based in Culver City specializing in events of all sizes. Whether you are looking for a romantic private date night, a family dinner or birthday party, a 200 person wedding guestlist, or a 500 person industry event, our staff will be there to cater to your every need. Working exclusively with vendors around the city, we provide a one-stop shop experience, so you can focus on more important things like getting excited for your event. Our team has the experience and creativity to facilitate any vision you may have to make your special day one to remember.
