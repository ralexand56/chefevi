Our Services

Personal Chef

You may book Chef Evi for a romantic date night, or an intmate dinner party in the comfort of your own home. She will bring everything you need to make your night one to remember.

Cooking Classes

From beginner to advanced cooks, Chef Evi is happy to provide you with the tools and knowlege to create whatever culinary masterpiece your heart desires.

Weekly Meal Prep

Are you too busy to cook, have dietary restrictions, or just want to eat healthier? Chef Evi will work with you on a menu and delivery schedule to keep you at your best all week long.

Events

We love to party! The Chef Evi Catering team primarily works with events, both small and large. Whether a small birthday party, coorporate event, industry shindig or massive wedding, our team would love to help you celebrate!

Food Truck

Chef Evi Catering is partnered with Green Truck in Culver City to make sure all of your needs are met. For an additional rental fee, you can have your very own food truck with any type of food you might want for your next outdoor event!

Rentals

We are exclusively partnered with a rental company based in Los Angeles to provide you with everything you need to make your event go off without a hitch. Because we are exclusive with this vendor, we tend to have more competitive pricing and we take care of everything so you can enjoy your event.
